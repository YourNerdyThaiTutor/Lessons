Lesson 10 Combo vowels

There are 21 vowels in Thai and there are 32 sounds

These are combinations of the other ones

Some vowels are standalones, the following are vowels made from combining the other vowels that we have already covered together. However, the sounds that they make are not necessarily the combination of those that they are made of, so don't get those mixed up.
