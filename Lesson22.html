Lesson 22 Shopping

We have adult money. Let's boost Thailand's GDP

Let's spend our adult money responsibly :) BUY EVERYTHING. I bought 26 bottles of fish sauce last trip. You can't get the good local stuff in America. My mom knows this fried fish person with their truck in front of the park we go to every morning. It was the best fish chips I've ever had in my entire life. My luggage was full of food and ingredients, but we ate it all in 2 weeks.

As you can see in a lot of examples, Thai people tend to skip the pronouns when talking about first or second person, only about third person if any. You can add in the pronouns, which we'll cover later, but it wouldn't sound natural to Thai people.
