Lesson 23 Hidden -ะ

"Scan" has 2 syllables in Thai

Sometimes there are words that look like this "สแกน" (scan, for when the vendors ask you to scan QR codes to pay). What are you supposed to do with the consonant that is just... alone? You add the -ะ vowel to the consonant, so it will sound like สะแกน.

There are 5 types of hidden -ะ

1. Leading hidden -ะ

The part that has the hidden -ะ is in the beginning of a word. Some words with single low consonant that supposed to have "ห" in front of the second syllable have to omit them. See Lesson 5. Otherwise, the words will be too croweded. Here are some examples, click on the buttons to hear the pronunciations. On the buttons are how the pronunciations are spelled out phonetically.

Word Definition Pronunciation
ขยะ trash
จมูก nose
สมุด notebook
องุ่น grape
ตลาด market
พยาบาล nurse
ถนน road
สบาย comfortable
สวัสดี hello
สถานี station
ตลก funny
ขนม snack
ขโมย steal

2. -อ เ- โ- leading

The beginning of a word has one of these 3 vowels. Then, follows by the hidden -ะ, such as.

Word Definition Pronunciation
เทวดา angel
โทรศัพท์ telephone
โฆษณา advertisement
ทรยศ betray
มรสุม storm

3. Double use of an ending consonant

The first part is pronouced normally , but the ending consonant of the first syllable doubles as a second syllable with -ะ. For example, สกปรก (dirty) is pronounced as สก กะ ปรก

Word Definition Pronunciation
สกปรก dirty
จักรยาน bicycle
สุขภาพ health
ผลิตภัณฑ์ product
จักจั่น cicada

4. Borrowed words

We are just sounding out foreign words and trying to fit into Thai. In English, "scan", the "sc" is just one sound, but in Thai, that sound does not exist, so the "s" and the "c" are pronouced separately.

Word Definition Pronunciation
สกี ski
สแปม spam
สแกน scan
สตรอว์เบอร์รี strawberry

5. Looks like a cluster

Sometimes you'll see that the consonants cluster together like a consonant cluster, but they're not one of the regular ones that can be pronouced together like a regular cluster like from Lesson 14. These will pronouce the first consonant with the -ะ and the second consonant with the vowel written. For example, แสตมป์ (stamp) is pronounced as สะ แตม

Word Definition Pronunciation
แสตมป์ stamp
เสนอ present/suggest
เกษตร argriculture
เผยอ ajar
