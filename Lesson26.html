Lesson 26 Flavors

Thai spicy is dangerous. Don't be overly confident.

For those who have not had Thai food in Thailand. It's not the same as Thai food in your country. Nowadays, maybe they'll ask for your spice tolerance. Still, some won't even ask. To save yourself from heartburn and burning bootyhole.

Example sentences

sentence translation pronunciation
เอาหวานน้อยค่ะ I'll take less sweet
เอาไม่ค่อยเผ็ดนะคะ Can I have not so spicy?
ขมไป ไม่อร่อย Too bitter, doesn't taste good.
ค่อนข้างเปรี้ยว Quite sour
มันไปหน่อย A little too oily
เค็มไม่พอ Not salty enough.
ไม่ค่อยนัว รสชาติแปลก Not so umami, tastes weird.
อร่อยมาก เค็มดี Very delicious, good kind of salty
เอาเผ็ดนิดหน่อย I'll take just a little spicy
เผ็ดมากไปไม่อร่อย Too spicy doesn't taste good.

You can use these as example for sentence structure, replace any flavor with other flavor that fits your situation. Maybe don't critique too much or you'll see some Muay Thai in action. Most of the time you'll just be adding มั้ย at the end of each word to ask if it is that flavor to know what the food tastes like. For example, หวานมั้ย = Is it sweet?
