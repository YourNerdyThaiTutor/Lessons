Lesson 23 ฤ ฦ ฤๅ ฦๅ

ฤ ฦ ฤๅ ฦๅ are vowels-ish

ฤ ฦ ฤๅ ฦๅ are these weird looking things at the end of your vowel list. They are vowels-ish. They have their own rules that you can't really apply vowel rules to them.

Once you learn a lot more vocabulary you'll see that no word uses ฦ or ฦๅ anymore, but some words still use ฤ and ฤๅ. At this level of Thai, we'll focus only on the words that you will use often but I'll leave a separate example table on the more poetic vocab just for those who are curious.

There are 3 different ways to pronounce "ฤ"

  • "ริ" : imagine if you replace the ฤ letter with ริ. This happens when ฤ follows ก ต ท ป ศ ส.

For example,

Word Meaning Pronunciation Is this a common word?
อังกฤษ England Yes, duh.
กฤษณา agarwood It can be; if you like plants.
ตฤษณา yearning No, this is purely to show you the rule.
ทฤษฏี theory Yes, when talking academically.
ปฤจฉา Question No, but if you want to get into grammar ปฤจฉาวิเศษณ์ means interrogative adverb and ปฤจฉาสรรพนาม means interrogative pronoun.
ศฤงคาร harem, good things in life No, unless you're into historical soap operas.
สฤต pass by, go through No

  • "รึ" : imagine if you replace the ฤ letter with รึ. This happens when ฤ follows ค น พ ม ห.

For example,

Word Meaning Pronunciation Is this a common word?
คฤหาสน์ Mansion Yes, if you know about rich people.
นฤมิต to create Yes, if you're into old literature.
หฤโหด mercilessly Yes, trauma :).
หฤหรรษ์ delight Yes, if you are hanging out with poetic people.
พฤกษ์ plant Yes, mostly about something that has to do with plants, not the plant itself. พฤกษศาสตร์ means botany. ศาสตร์ means the study of.
พฤศจิก Scorpio Yes, to all the astrology girlies.
พฤศจิกายน November Yes
พฤษภ Taurus Yes


A lot of silent borrowed words are written with no tone mark, but will be pronounced with the tones pronounced in the original language, or what Thai people could decipher.

Some words like พระจันทร์ has 2 silent letters which are ท and ร. It would only be pronounced as พระจัน. If there are multiple ending consonants, just pronounce the first letter and leave the rest silent.

If you wonder why รถเมล์ is called รถเมล์ and how it is borrowed from English, there's a mini history lesson. Back in the day, the only public cars were own by the post office to deliver mail so they called the first public transport bus a mail car. รถ means car เมล์ is mail. Today, we still call public bus รถเมล์ although they don't deliver mail anymore.

เบอร์ is used for numbers as in phone number, a wait line number, or numbers used in any identification, not numbers for calculations. For calculations we'll use เลข
