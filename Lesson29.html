Lesson 29 Places

Don't get lost.

Here are the vocab for places. These next few lessons are just going to be vocab dumps. Well, if you don't know the words, you can't build a sentence. In Thai, grammar is not that important. There are certain cases when it's important. However, for now, learn all these words and it'll help you out. Most of the times Thai people just throw words out there and hope for the best.

Example sentences

places
sentence translation pronunciation
โรงพยาบาลอยู่ไหนคะ Where is the hospital?
ธนาคารไปทางไหนคะ Which way is the bank?
แถวนี้มีป้ายรถเมล์ไหมคะ Is there a bus stop around here?

You can use (place)+อยู่ไหน for "Where is (place)?" for all of the cases, but if you want the general direction of where to head to use (place)+ไปทางไหน for "Which way is the (place)?". If you want to ask if there is a (place) around here somewhere, you can use แถวนี้มี+(place)+ไหม
