Lesson 29 Places

Don't get lost.

Here are the vocab for places. These few lessons are just going to be vocab dumps. We'll if you don't know the words, you can't build a sentence. In Thai, grammar is not that important. There are certain cases when it's important. However, for now, learn all these words and it'll help you out. Most of the times Thai people just throw words out there and hope for the best.
