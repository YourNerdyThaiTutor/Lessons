Lesson 31 รร

รร It's just like ะ but more complicated.

We already have ะ. Why do we need another way to write it? It's because Thai is a dramatic diva and she needs to feel special with her high maintenance rules to keep you on your toes.

There are 2 ways to use รร.

The first one, if there is no ending consonant, it is pronounced like it is "-ัน". For example,

word meaning pronunciation
กรรไกร scissors
ภรรยา wife
บรรทัด line (of writing)
บรรณารักษ์ librarian
บรรจุ contain, hold, pack
บรรยาย narrate
อัศจรรย์ miraculous, marvel
สวรรค์ heaven, paradise
มหัศจรรย์ amazing, astonishing

The second way, if there is an ending consonant, use it as "-ั" + the ending consonant. For example,

word meaning pronunciation
กรรม karma
ธรรมชาติ nature
พรรคพวก friends, partisans
รัฐธรรมนูญ constitution
สรรพคุณ properties, qualities, effects
อุปสรรค obstacle
นิทรรศการ exhibition

Once you get used to it, it's not too bad. The fact that this exists as another rule to remember is just annoying.
