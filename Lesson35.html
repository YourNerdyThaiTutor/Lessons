Lesson 35 Astrological signs

Is this important to my survival? Probably not.

It's not important, but I already had you learn the months and it's already in your brains, hopefully, might as well learn these too. I know some of you are eager to ask people in the clubs about their signs.
Anyways, once you know the months, it's pretty easy to know the astrological zodiac. The constellations themselves are just the name of the month before the vowel "า" in most cases, but who cares about constellations. The good stuff are your zodiac signs. It's very close to the constellations' naming convention, just a few tweaks.
Everything will begin with ราศี which just means astrological zodiac. All of them will be similar to the word for each month, so to not confuse you, I'll start with the one for January

word meaning pronunciation

ราศีกุมภ์ Aquarius

Example sentences

sentence translation pronunciation
คุณราศีอะไรคะ What is your sign?
ราศีตุลค่ะ I'm a Libra.
ฉันราศีเมษค่ะ I'm an Aries.
แฟนเก่าฉันราศีเมถุน My ex is a Gemini.
อยากได้แฟนใหม่ราศีเมษ I want a new girlfriend/boyfriend who's an Aries ;)
