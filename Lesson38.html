Lesson 38 Family

Everybody is family in Thailand.

It's true, everyone is family in Thailand. You go to the market, and suddenly you gained 5 moms, 3 dads, 4 uncles, 32 aunties, and who's child is this? Why is she on my leg? If you see a stranger, it's very normal to call them as if they're family. Just pick one outof the family tree that looks suitable for that person. If they're a little older than you, call them พี่. If they look older than your mom, call them ป้า or ลุง. But for infinity points, call the old ladies พี่คนสวย, and the old uncles พี่สุดหล่อ. No matter what you did wrong, it will all be forgiven.
