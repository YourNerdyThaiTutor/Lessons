Lesson 39 Fruits

They are delicious, but don't cut your legs off.

When you go to Thailand, of course you need to eat the fruits. It's the best. A lot of the things in this list are tropical fruits that are common in Thailand, but might not be common in your home country (idk where you're from). So...if you don't know what it looks like, look it up. You got skills. I believe in you.

Just a warning, my mom got her diabetes from eating 10 kilos or fruits everyday. We tried to tell her to stop doing that for years, but she didn't listen until half of her face was paralyzed.

There is a saying that goes หวานตัดขา, which means it's so sweet I will have to cut off my legs. It's a reference to how diabetes destroys your nerves, a lot of the times in your legs. When it gets so bad you have to amputate it. Watch your sugar intake. Eat in moderation. Anyways, enjoy your fruits.
