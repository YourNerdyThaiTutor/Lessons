Lesson 43 At the doctor

Try to survive, will ya?

Usually, doctors are the smartest people you will meet, so they will most likely speak English, especially if you go to those fancy private hospitals. However, nurses don't always speak English if you somehow ended up in a small hospital in the middle of nowhere. Here's how you will survive.
